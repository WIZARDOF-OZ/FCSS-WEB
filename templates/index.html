    A Legacy Of Vision

    The religious institute of the Sisters of our Lady of Fatima, which has a Generalate in Pune was founded in 1893, in Bellary-Karnataka, by Mgr. Francis Xavier Kroot, a Mill Hill Missionary from Holland.

    His intense love and zeal to spread the Kingdom of God inspired him to work for all, especially for the liberation of the down trodden and particularly women.

    The vision of our founder impels us to be educators in joy and hope to build a society of persons who are caring and responsible. It is therefore, our privilege to take up the challenge educating the youth with the love and compassion of Jesus Christ, The Supreme Teacher.

    We endeavour to inculcate in our students the Gospel Values of love, sharing and caring, forgiveness, equality of labour and human life, in order that they may live simple and meaningful lives, with a spirit of patriotism and be promoters of justice, peace and harmony as responsible citizens.
